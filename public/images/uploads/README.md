## Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended size: 500x300 pixels or higher
- Aspect ratio: 5:3 (landscape) or 4:3 (portrait)
- File size: Under 5MB for optimal performance

## How to Add Images:
1. Place your images in this folder (`public/images/uploads/`)
2. Use descriptive filenames (no spaces, use hyphens)
3. The dashboard will automatically detect and display your images
4. You can organize them by categories using the naming convention above

## Tips for Great Photos:
- Use good lighting (natural light works best)
- Keep the subject clear and focused
- Consider the composition and framing
- Include people or context to make photos more engaging
- Take multiple shots and choose the best ones

## Local Storage Features:
- Your image preferences are saved locally
- Add titles and descriptions through the dashboard
- Organize images by personal categories
- Rate and favorite your best photos
- All data stays on your device for privacy
