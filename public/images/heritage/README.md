## Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended size: 500x300 pixels or higher
- Aspect ratio: 5:3 (landscape)
- File size: Under 2MB for optimal performance

## Where to Find Images:
- Local photography
- Free stock photos from Unsplash, Pexels, or Pixabay
- Government tourism websites
- Municipal corporation archives
