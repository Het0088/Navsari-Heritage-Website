## Image Requirements:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended size: 500x300 pixels or higher
- Aspect ratio: 5:3 (landscape)
- File size: Under 2MB for optimal performance

## Tips for Good Food Photography:
- Use natural lighting when possible
- Show the food attractively plated
- Include some context (vendor stall, restaurant setting)
- Make the food look fresh and appealing
- Consider showing the preparation process

## Where to Find Images:
- Local food photography
- Visit actual vendors and take photos
- Food blogs and recipe websites
- Free stock photos from Unsplash, Pexels, or Pixabay
- Social media with proper attribution
